Virtual Summer Workshop 2023: Week 4

Make your water future with Minecraft

Minecraft is an open world game that allows players to build and explore. In this workshop, we will use Minecraft to explore the water cycle and how we can use water wisely. We will also learn how to use the Python programming language to create our own Minecraft worlds.
