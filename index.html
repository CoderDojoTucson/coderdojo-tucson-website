Welcome to CoderDojo Tucson!

It's time for the Games for Change Student Challenge!

Join CoderDojo Tucson for weekly worskhops to prepare your entry for this year's challenge. The challenge is open to students in grades 5-12.

We'll meet on Wednesdays from 3:30-5:30pm, starting on 2 October. Some weeks will be virtual meetings and at least one meeting a month will be in person. Click the button below for the detailed schedule, to be posted soon!
