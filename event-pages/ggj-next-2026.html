GGJ Next Tucson 2026

A game jam for ages 10-18

CoderDojo Tucson is hosting a hybrid GGJ Next event from 31 January—28 March 2026!

GGJ Next is a game jam for young people ages 10-18. It's a chance to learn about game design and development, meet new people, and have fun.

Workshop Schedule

All of this year's workshops will be from 3-5pm MST.
Virtual meetings will be online at the Google Meet link in the Google Classroom. Hybrid meetings will be in-person at Martha Cooper Library and online in the Google Classroom.

  1. Saturday, January 31st - Hybrid: GGJ Next Tucson Kickoff
  2. Friday, February 6th - Virtual: Intro to game design
  3. Friday, February 13th - Virtual: Game development tools
  4. Saturday, February 21st - Hybrid: Prototyping
  5. Wednesday, February 25th - Virtual: Playtesting and feedback
  6. Wednesday, March 4th - Virtual: Focus on game play
  7. Friday, March 13th - Virtual: Enhancing your game
  8. Saturday, March 21st - Hybrid: Final playtest and polish
  9. Saturday, March 28th - Hybrid: Demo and submission

CoderDojo Tucson is a dojo of the CoderDojo Foundation, which is part of the Raspberry Pi Foundation.
