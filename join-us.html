Join CoderDojo Tucson

CoderDojo is a global, volunteer-led movement of free, open coding clubs (Dojos) for young people aged 7 to 17!

Currently, we hold most of our events virtually using Google Meet. We do periodically hold special events in-person at the Southern Arizona Arts and Cultural Alliance (SAACA) Catalyst Arts and Maker Space in the Mall of Tucson.

Want to be part of the fun? Look for event announcements and register to participate on our CoderDojo Community page.

CoderDojo Tucson is a dojo of the CoderDojo Foundation.
