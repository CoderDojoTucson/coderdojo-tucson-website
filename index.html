Game Design Workshops!!!

For the 2024-25 school year, we'll be working on creating games related to the Sustainable Development Goals.

There are two competitions you can create a game for:

  • NSF Game Maker Awards
  • Games for Change Student Challenge

We'll meet on the first Wednesday of the month from 3:30-5:30pm in the multipurpose room of the newly remodeled Martha Cooper Library.
