Summer Workshop 2024

This summer CoderDojo Tucson will be partnering with Watershed Management Group for two one-week workshops. The workshops will be held at the Living Lab and Learning Center Monday-Friday from 9am-12pm on the weeks of 10-14 June and 24-28 June 2024.

Week 1: Do your :bit Water Challenge

10-14 June 2024

We'll take a deep dive (pun intended) into water harvesting basins and you'll have an opportunity by the end of the week to create your own water harvesting project with a micro:bit.

Week 2: Native Trees and Plants

24-28 June 2024

Did you know we are surrounded by a bounty of edible foods from the trees and plants native to the Sonoran Desert?

This week we'll learn more about our native edible trees in Tucson and then you can use that inspiration to tell others. You can design a game, build an app to map locations of edible trees, animate a story, or whatever else you are inspired to create.

You supply the inspiration, we'll provide the support!

CoderDojo Tucson is a dojo of the CoderDojo Foundation, which is part of the Raspberry Pi Foundation.
