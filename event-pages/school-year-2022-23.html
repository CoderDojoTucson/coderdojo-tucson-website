Weekly dojo meetings for 2022/23

Work on the projects you want to make!

A new school year has begun and that means it's time to return to our weekly virtual dojo meetings. Join us online every Wednesday from 4-6pm MST to work on projects you want to create.

Here are a few of the things we'd like to work on this year in the dojo.

  • Scratch
  • Game programs with GameGo
  • Game programs with Pico System
  • Wearable tech with Circuit Playground Express
  • Physical computing with the STARTO Starter Kit
  • Physical computing with the Inventor 2040 W
  • Linux on the Raspberry Pi 3
  • and more!

You'll get the tools and help you need to build your projects and we'll be meeting in a fun virtual space in Gather Town!

CoderDojo Tucson is a dojo of the CoderDojo Foundation, which is part of the Raspberry Pi Foundation.
