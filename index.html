Welcome to CoderDojo Tucson!

CoderDojo Tucson is the Tucson, Arizona dojo of the international CoderDojo.

CoderDojo Tucson is a place where teens can bring their ideas or just a motivation to learn new tech skills and have a safe and supportive environment to pursue their objectives. We provide equipment, support, and adult mentoring.

CoderDojo Tucson currently meets weekly from 1-3 every Wednesday in the 101Space at the Joel D Valdez Main Library in downtown Tucson.

Here are some of the technologies you can experiment with:

  • 3D printing
  • Augmented/Virtual reality
  • Game programming
  • Web programming
  • Mobile device programming
  • Robotics
  • Raspberry Pi
  • Arduino

Created in semantic HTML5. Feel free to look at the code!
