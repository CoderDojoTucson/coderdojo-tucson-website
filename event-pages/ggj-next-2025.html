GGJ Next 2025 Tucson

A game jam for ages 12-17

CoderDojo Tucson will be hosting an hybrid GGJ Next event from 20-26 January 2025!

GGJ Next is a game jam for young people ages 12-17. It's a chance to learn about game design and development, meet new people, and have fun.

Register for the GGJ Next Google Classroom here.

Schedule

  • Day 1 (Monday, 20 Jan 4-6pm) - Virtual: What's a game jam?
    • Google Classroom
  • Day 1 (Monday, 20 Jan 7-8pm) - Virtual: GGJ theme reveal
    • Google Classroom and Tucson Game Dev Discord
  • Day 2 (Tuesday, 21 Jan 4-6pm) - Virtual: What makes a game fun?
    • Google Classroom
  • Day 3 (Wednesday, 22 Jan 4-6pm) - Hybrid: Game design
    • Martha Cooper Library and Google Classroom
  • Day 4 (Thursday, 23 Jan 4-6pm) - Virtual: Game engines
    • Google Classroom
  • Day 5 (Friday, 24 Jan 4-6pm) - Virtual: Game assets
    • Google Classroom
  • Day 6 (Saturday, 25 Jan 1-4pm) - Hybrid: Work on your game
    • UA Gould-Simpson Bldg and Google Classroom
  • Day 7 (Sunday, 26 Jan 2-4pm) - Hybrid: Game demos
    • UA Gould-Simpson Bldg and Tucson Game Dev Discord

CoderDojo Tucson is a dojo of the CoderDojo Foundation, which is part of the Raspberry Pi Foundation.
