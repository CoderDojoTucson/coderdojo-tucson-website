GGJ Next 2024 Tucson

A game jam for ages 12-17

CoderDojo Tucson will be hosting an online GGJ Next event from 22-28 January 2024!

GGJ Next is a game jam for young people ages 12-17. It's a chance to learn about game design and development, meet new people, and have fun.

Here are some of the things we'll be doing:

  • Day 1 (22 Jan): What's a game jam?
  • Day 2 (23 Jan): What makes a game fun?
  • Day 3 (24 Jan): Game Design
  • Day 4 (25 Jan): Independent work on your game design
  • Day 5 (26 Jan): Game Engines
  • Day 6 (27 Jan): Independent work on your game
  • Day 7 (28 Jan): Finish Your Game

CoderDojo Tucson is a dojo of the CoderDojo Foundation, which is part of the Raspberry Pi Foundation.
