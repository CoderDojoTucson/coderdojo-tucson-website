In-person Summer Workshop 2023: Week 6

Connect with native flora and fauna

Learn about plants (flora) and animals (fauna) native to the Sonoran Desert through a number of interactive projects at the Living Lab and Learning Center.

Here is the detailed schedule for this week:

  • Monday, 17 July: Plants and animals of the Sonoran Desert
    • Learn about the plants and animals native to the Sonoran Desert
    • Tour the Living Lab to see how we support plants and animals using local water resources
    • Investigate the role plants and animals play in our watershed
  • Tuesday, 18 July: Phenology - the life cycle of plants
    • Learn about phenology (the life cycle of plants)
    • Monitor the phenology of plants at the Living Lab using Nature's Notebook
    • Use augmented reality and mapping tools to map plants at the Living Lab
  • Wednesday, 19 July: Beavers!
    • What do beavers do for us?
    • Beaver reintroduction and surveys
    • Make a survey monitor using micro:bits
  • Thursday, 20 July: Bats and wildlife
    • Which bats can we see in the Sonoran Desert?
    • How can we help create habitat for bats?
    • Experiment with wildlife cameras
  • Friday, 21 July: Flora and Fauna game day
    • Play a game that teaches about flora and fauna in the Sonoran Desert
    • Make a game that teaches about flora and fauna in the Sonoran Desert
