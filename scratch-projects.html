Scratch Projects

A project created by one of the CoderDojo Tucson ninjas for the Games for Change Student Challenge 2023. Try it out!

Instructions: This is a platformer game about climate change! Avoid the obstacles like oil, compost, and plastic. The first time you touch an obstacle on a level, a message about how that obstacle contributes to climate change will pop up. Click on the message to make it disappear. Use the right, left, and up arrows to navigate.

CoderDojo Tucson is a dojo of the CoderDojo Foundation.
